Splunk Search Head Clustering (3 SH + 1 Deployer)

Upload your PEM key, enter host IPs, and deploy Splunk 10.0.0 to /opt/splunk.

1) Deploy SH Cluster

PEM stays on this machine under uploads/.
If unchecked, Splunk will elect a captain automatically.

2) Deploy an App (folder) via Deployer

This creates $SPLUNK_HOME/etc/shcluster/apps/<app> on the deployer and pushes the bundle to the captain.
